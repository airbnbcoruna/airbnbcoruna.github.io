A Coruña Airport

A Coruña Airport is located 8 kilometres outside of the city and it mostly deals with domestic flights. The buses at Coruña Airport arrive and depart just outside the terminal building. You can also fly to Santiago de Compostela to reach A Coruña.

Public Transport

You can easily walk to most places in A Coruña, but if you are thinking about spending a bit of time in the city you can also choose to travel by bus or tram. The local bus system is well organized and provides a reliable service.
